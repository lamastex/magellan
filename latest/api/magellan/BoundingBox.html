case class BoundingBox(xmin: Double, ymin: Double, xmax: Double, ymax: Double) extends Product with Serializable

A bounding box is an axis parallel rectangle. It is completely specified by the bottom left and top right points.

xmin

the minimum x coordinate of the rectangle.

ymin

the minimum y coordinate of the rectangle.

xmax

the maximum x coordinate of the rectangle.

ymax

the maximum y coordinate of the rectangle.
